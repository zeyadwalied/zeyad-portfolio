Services

Custom WooCommerce solutions and WordPress engineering built around revenue, speed, and scale.

If your store needs custom product logic, plugin development, better performance, or technical cleanup before scaling, these are the core service lanes I handle.

Custom WordPress Plugins WooCommerce Development Performance and Scaling
Service 01

Custom WordPress Plugin Development

Problem: Businesses often try to force unique workflows into generic plugins, which leads to feature bloat, conflicts, poor admin UX, and fragile maintenance.

Solution: I build custom WordPress plugins that match the exact workflow you need, whether that means custom admin tools, pricing logic, automations, integrations, or storefront behavior.

Outcome: Cleaner code, fewer plugin conflicts, easier editing, and a system that supports your business model instead of fighting it.

Service 02

WooCommerce Development

Problem: Many WooCommerce stores lose sales because product configuration, checkout, upsells, or customer journeys feel slow, confusing, or too limited.

Solution: I customize WooCommerce flows with better product experiences, custom cart and checkout logic, store automation, and conversion-focused improvements that align with how your customers actually buy.

Outcome: Better user flow, stronger conversion potential, improved store flexibility, and more control over how the sales experience works.

Service 03

Performance Optimization

Problem: Slow WooCommerce stores burn ad spend, reduce trust, and create drop-off during product browsing or checkout.

Solution: I audit theme and plugin overhead, reduce unnecessary requests, improve frontend delivery, and fix the common bottlenecks that make WordPress and WooCommerce feel heavy.

Outcome: Faster load times, smoother browsing, better conversion conditions, and a stronger technical base for SEO and paid traffic.

Service 04

Bug Fixing and Scaling

Problem: Stores that grow quickly often accumulate checkout bugs, integration issues, plugin incompatibilities, and unstable custom code.

Solution: I diagnose the root cause, fix the current issue, and improve the structure around it so the same bottleneck does not keep coming back with every new campaign or feature request.

Outcome: More stable releases, fewer urgent breakdowns, and a WooCommerce setup that can support larger catalog, traffic, and operational complexity.
